Boise's Premier Skateshop



After working in skate retail for about six years, Paul Whitworth was ready for a change and set out to do things his way. In 2004, Whitworth opened Prestige Skateboards, a 600-square-foot shop located in downtown Boise, with a mission to build a shop that would cater to locals, he says. “I wanted something accessible to the downtown area. There is a skatepark down the street, and I really wanted something people could skate to.” Together with co-owner and snowboard legend Greg Goulet, Whitworth began making a name for the shop, hosting several skate deck design contests with local artists and skaters, and today the shop is recognized as one of the staples in the local skate scene.
